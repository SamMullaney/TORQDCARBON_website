About TORQD CARBON

Torqd Carbon specializes in carbon fiber steering wheels for performance cars. The steering wheel is the only part of your car you constantly touch and look at, that's why we offer a wide range of inventory as well as custom wheels if we don't have your brand/style on our website. How does that work? We send steering wheel frames to our manufacturer to be sanded and refinished with lighter, stronger, sportier materials. Customize your ride today and Torq out!

Why order from us?

All wheels come standard with carbon fiber magnetic paddle shifters (multiple exchangeable styles depending on model) as well as wheel covers, buttons, and airbag

All wheels come with a 6 month warranty and can be replaced for free if you provide a valid reason

Our quality stands out from the stitching on the grips to the clicks on the steering wheel.

Frequently Asked Questions

What sets us apart from other brands?

We hand make every single steering wheel with the finest materials. 3k carbon fiber, real Alcantara, perforated leather, smooth leather, and more!

What do turnaround times look like?

Expect your product within 3-5 weeks after ordering, but as soon as it arrives it's plug and play (10-45 minutes depending on person)

What's the purpose of a wheel cover?

Wheel covers add an aesthetic look to the wheel when you aren't driving and protect your steering wheel from the sun while keeping it cool.
